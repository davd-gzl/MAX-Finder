A free, open-source, serverless finder for trains where a MAX JEUNE or MAX
SENIOR (ex-TGVmax) seat is currently reservable — so you don't have to probe
station-by-station in SNCF Connect.

The hard part — which trains actually have a free MAX seat — is published by SNCF
as open data. MAX Finder is a frontend over that dataset: no backend, no account,
no cost, no tracking. Your favorites, settings and searches live only on your
device.

Features:

* Où partir — every destination reachable with a free MAX seat from your station,
  ranked by how many MAX trains run there over the booking window.
* D'où venir — the reverse: every origin that can reach a destination.
* Trajet précis — full origin to destination search with a 30-day availability
  calendar and a round-trip / weekend-getaway finder.
* Idées — the fastest destinations reachable from your station that day.
* Tour — a multi-city, day-by-day itinerary chaining free MAX seats.
* Multi-leg connections via hubs, with optional stopover and overnight modes.
* Map of every reachable station, filters (time, duration, train type, region),
  calendar export (ICS), and shareable links.
* 11 languages, light/dark theme, offline support.

This is an independent tool. It is not affiliated with SNCF, does not sell
tickets, and availability is indicative — always confirm and book on SNCF Connect.
